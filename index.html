Hello everyone! My name is Ayana Amin, and I am a fourth-year student at Northeastern University studying Economics and Business Administration with a concentration in Finance and a minor in Data Science. I’m originally from New Jersey, but I’ve really enjoyed spending the past few years in Boston and getting to explore everything the city has to offer. During my first year of college, I had the opportunity to study abroad in London through Northeastern N.U. Bound Program. It was an incredible experience that allowed me to step outside of my bubble, meet people from different backgrounds, experience a new culture, and travel throughout Europe. Traveling is something I really enjoy, and I try to take advantage of opportunities to visit new places whenever I can. On campus, I’m the fundraising chair for the Women in Business Club and the treasurer of EconPress, Northeastern’s economic journal. Professionally, I have completed two co-ops: my first was at Biogen in Internal Audit, and my second was at State Street as a Corporate Risk and Compliance Analyst. Outside of academics and work, I enjoy going to the gym, listening to pop and rap music, going to concerts, and spending time with my friends. I am also a huge foodie and love to try new restaurants around Boston. My favorite cuisines are Thai and Japanese, and I’m always excited to find new spots to try. I’m excited to continue learning, meeting new people, and gaining new perspectives this semester.
